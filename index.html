What counts as Basic vs Special luck?

  • Basic (additive percentages): gear % (right-hand), Lucky/Fortune potions, Lucky Penny, strange potions' luck%, Stella candles, Tip's Blessing, Basic Blessing, area tools (Portable Device, Incense), tiny Resonance boosts, many event/server buffs.
  • Bonus Roll (multiplier): from left-hand gear like Gravitational (×6), Blessed Tide (×3), or Flesh (×2). Only multiplies Basic luck.
  • Special (flat luck): Bound (+50k), Heavenly (+15k/+150k), Godlike (+400k), Oblivion (+600k), Pump King's Blood (+700k). Added outside Bonus, then multiplied by VIP.
  • VIP: multiplies the final result (VIP ×1.2, VIP+ ×1.3).
